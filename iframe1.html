Telugu superstar Rana Daggubati who also has numerous business interests from producing movies, to SFX studios is making a foray into the Metaverse with his newly launched company Ikonz. Ikonz will hold the intellectual property rights that Rana owns through his various businesses - from legacy movies under the Suresh Productions banner, to iconic brands like Amar Chitra Katha and Tinkle Studios. Rana chats with Rahul Jagtiani where we discloses his ideas on the Metaverse and his interest and association with blockchain technology. Watch this engaging interview.
